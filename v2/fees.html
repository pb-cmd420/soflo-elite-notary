How Travel Fees Work: Your total travel fee = Distance zone rate x Traffic multiplier. We calculate the distance from our location to yours, then apply the multiplier based on time of day. South Florida traffic is no joke — a 15-mile trip at noon can take 50+ minutes during rush hour on I-95 or US-1. Travel fees are always quoted before your appointment. After-hours, weekend, and holiday surcharges are added separately.
