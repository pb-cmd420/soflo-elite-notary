Still Have Questions?

Frequently asked questions.

How much does a mobile notary cost?
Florida caps notarial acts at $10 each. Travel is by distance zone ($15–$50+) with a traffic multiplier; after-hours, weekend, or holiday surcharges may apply. You'll always get a transparent total quoted before the appointment.
What do I need to bring?
A valid, government-issued photo ID. Do not sign the document beforehand — the notary must witness your signature. Have it complete but unsigned when we arrive.
What are your hours?
Available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week — including nights, weekends, and holidays. Same-day appointments are often available with advance notice.
