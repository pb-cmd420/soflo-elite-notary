Scope & limitations: As a Florida Notary Public we cannot issue apostilles (only the Florida Secretary of State can), translate documents, process passport applications, issue permits or licenses, draft legal documents or give legal advice, notarize our own signature, or certify vital records (birth/death/marriage certificates). If a request is outside a notary's authority, we will tell you up front and point you in the right direction.
