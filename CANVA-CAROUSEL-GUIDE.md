## 10. Instagram Carousel Best Practices (2026)

### Why Carousels Work
- Carousels average **1.92% engagement rate** vs 0.50% for Reels and 0.45% for single images
- Carousels drive **22% more saves** than single photos — saves are heavily weighted by the algorithm
- DM shares from carousels are weighted **3–5× higher than likes**
- Adding "swipe left →" prompt on slide 1 increases engagement from 1.83% → 2.00%

### Optimal Format (Canva settings)
| Setting | Value |
|---------|-------|
| **Canvas size** | 1080 × 1350 px (4:5 portrait) — takes 25% more feed space than square |
| **Alternative** | 1080 × 1080 px (1:1 square) — safe for profile grid |
| **Slide count** | 7–10 slides (sweet spot for dwell time) |
| **Max slides** | 20 (Instagram limit) |
| **File format** | PNG for text/graphics, JPG for photos |
| **Safe zone** | Keep critical text within center 1080×1080px square |

### Slide Structure (proven formula)
| Slide | Purpose | Rule |
|-------|---------|------|
| **1 — Hook** | Stop the scroll | Bold claim, question, or surprising stat. Include "swipe →" text |
| **2 — Problem** | Agitate the pain | Why this matters to them |
| **3–7 — Value** | Deliver the goods | One idea per slide. Max 30 words. Big text, lots of space |
| **8–9 — Proof** | Build trust | Credential, testimonial, or stat |
| **Last slide — CTA** | Convert | One clear action: call, DM, link in bio |

### Design Rules for Canva
1. **One idea per slide** — if you can't say it in 2 short lines, split it into two slides
2. **30-word max per slide** — viewers swipe fast
3. **Big font** — minimum 28pt for body, 40pt+ for headlines
4. **Consistent template** — same colors, same fonts every carousel (saves time, builds brand recognition)
5. **High contrast** — dark background + gold or white text (your brand already does this well)
6. **Slide 1 must work as a standalone image** — it appears in the feed before the swipe
7. **Brand your last slide** — logo, handle (@sofloelitenotary), phone, CTA button
8. **PNG export** — for text-heavy carousels, always export as PNG, not JPG

### Caption Formula
```
Line 1: Repeat the hook from slide 1 (drives saves from caption alone)
Line 2-3: 1-2 sentences expanding the value
.
.
.
Line 4: CTA — "DM us to book" / "Link in bio" / "Call (786) 655-8681"
Line 5: Hashtags (5–15, mix broad + niche)
```

## 12. Quick Canva Workflow

1. **Set up one master template** — 1080×1350px, `#1E1E1E` background, gold + white text, your logo bottom-right
2. **Create 3 slide layouts:**
   - Hook slide (big bold headline, centered)
   - Content slide (headline + 3-4 bullet points or short paragraph)
   - CTA slide (logo + phone + "Book Now" button in gold)
3. **Duplicate the template** for each new carousel — never start from scratch
4. **Export as PNG** — File → Download → PNG
5. **Upload all slides** as a single carousel post (order matters — slide 1 is the hook)
